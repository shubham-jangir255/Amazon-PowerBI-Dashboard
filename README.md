# Amazon-Products-Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

🛒 **Amazon Insights: Product Performance & Pricing Analytics Dashboard**

A dynamic, interactive Power BI report built to explore Amazon product data — focusing on category performance, pricing trends, ratings, and discount strategies.

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Amazon Insights Dashboard is a visually engaging and analytical Power BI report designed to help users explore and compare Amazon product listings across multiple categories. The dashboard focuses on highlighting key product metrics like price, discount percentage, ratings, and review volume. This tool is intended for use by business analysts, e-commerce strategists, category managers, and data-driven marketers who seek to understand pricing trends and customer sentiment across the Amazon product catalog.

## 3. Tech Stack

List of the key technologies used to build the dashboard:

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
- 📁 **Power Query** – Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.
- 🧮 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures, dynamic visuals, and conditional logic.
- 📝 **Data Modeling** – Relationships established among tables (products, categories, and ratings) to enable cross-filtering and accurate aggregation.
- 📁 **File Format** – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

## 5. Features / Highlights

The best dashboard explanation format.

- Business problem
- Goal of the dashboard
- Walk through of key visuals (briefly!)

### Example:

**Business Problem**

The Amazon marketplace lists millions of products across categories, yet sellers and analysts often lack a clear view of how pricing, discounting, and ratings interact to drive customer purchase decisions across product categories.

**Key questions such as:**
- Which categories offer the highest discounts?
- Which products have the best rating-to-price ratio?
- What categories have the most reviewed products?
- Compare the highest and lowest priced products to understand category positioning and potential premium segments.

- **Price vs Discount Analysis (Scatter/Bar Chart)**

Chart showing:
- Actual price
- Discounted price
- Discount percentage
- Rating count

This helps users gauge pricing strategy and discount aggressiveness across categories.

## 6. Business Impact & Insights

- **Pricing Strategy:** Sellers can use this dashboard to identify optimal discount ranges for different categories (budget vs premium segments).
- **Category Expansion:** Business teams can spot underserved or high-rated categories with growth potential.
- **Customer Sentiment Tracking:** Marketers can plan campaigns based on rating trends, review volume, and price sensitivity.
- **Competitive Analysis:** Analysts can benchmark category performance by comparing pricing and discount patterns across the catalog.
